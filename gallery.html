World 9 Gaming Conventions: Megaman Bubbleman Style!

Inspired by the world of Megaman in the Bubbleman level, the retro gaming convention company, World 9 Gaming, has set up video game rooms for conventions across the Midwest and beyond. I created this web map using Mapbox to publish tilesets with custom icons for those Megaman enthusiasts.
