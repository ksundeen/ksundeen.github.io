Survey Results: Women in Tech (WiT) | generalized responses (April 2018)

WiT's Mission: To inspire and engage more women in all ages and career stages in topics of professional development related to technology. More specifically, we aim to 1) network with fellow women in technology fields, 2) provide a supportive environment for learning, creativity, mentorship, and professional development, and 3) discuss new initiatives, jobs, and ideas for how to retain our talent locally.
